About Lumo

A Saudi brand specialized in economical interior design and furnishing, established in 2022. It targets real estate developers and property investors to help maximize their sales and rental returns. Lumo has built a strong alliance between affordable pricing and high quality in both design and execution resulting in a powerful combination that clearly serves the interests of developers and investors.

CEO’s Message

Since Lumo was founded in 2022, we set out with a clear vision: to provide interior design and furnishing solutions that combine cost-efficiency with high quality — becoming a true partner for real estate developers and investors. At Lumo, we believe that smart design and professional execution don’t have to come at a high cost. With a clear vision, practical expertise, and a strong alliance between competitive pricing and top-tier quality, we’ve proven that this formula is not only possible — it’s essential in today’s real estate market. We are passionate about being part of our clients’ success, and we are constantly developing our services to meet the evolving needs of the market — always placing client satisfaction and return on investment at the heart of every project we deliver. On behalf of the entire Lumo team, I extend my sincere gratitude to everyone who has placed their trust in us. We remain committed to moving forward with ambition and confidence toward a more innovative and impactful future.

CEO.
Abdullah Khalid Al-Subaih
